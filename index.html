Case Study — Elevato Studios

One Person.
Every Layer.

Elevato Studios is a direct-to-consumer apparel label built on Shopify. As Website Developer & E-Commerce Operations Manager, I design the storefront, build and maintain the theme, and run the day-to-day commerce operations — from product launches to fulfillment policy.

01 — The Brief

A lean brand, running lean

Elevato sells knitwear, tees, and shorts under the line "Style Without Effort," shipping to customers across more than 60 countries with localized currency at checkout. There's no in-house design or dev team beyond me — the same person shaping the homepage also configures the return policy and updates the promo code.

02 — What I Built

Storefront, theme to detail

New Arrivals rail

Dynamic collection carousel with color/size variant swapping, built in Liquid.

Brand story section

Custom grid-overlay treatment for the "About Us" module on the homepage.

Editorial merchandising blocks

Full-bleed lifestyle sections tying seasonal photography to shop-now CTAs.

Newsletter capture

Popup + footer signup flow feeding the brand's email list.

03 — Running the Shop

Where dev meets operations

Catalog
Manage the product catalog and collection structure — New Arrivals, Tops, Bottoms, Shop All — as inventory and drops rotate.
Checkout
Configured for international commerce: multi-currency pricing across 60+ countries, Shop Pay, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and card processors.
Fulfillment
Built out shipping, refund, and returns policy pages, including a self-serve returns portal for customers.
Marketing ops
Run seasonal promotions and discount codes (e.g. the site's current sitewide offer), plus the newsletter capture flow end to end.
